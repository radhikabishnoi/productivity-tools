Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we handle your information.

Introduction

At ProductivityTools.in, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and tools. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

Important: Our tools are designed to work without requiring any personal information. We believe in privacy by design.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect almost no personal data. Since our tools work without registration, we don't require your name, email, or any personal information to use them.

1.2 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data through services like Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This includes:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website (if any)
  • General location (country/city level)

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

1.3 Local Storage

Some tools might store data temporarily in your browser's local storage for functionality (like saving your to-do list or meeting notes). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Can be cleared by you at any time
  • Is not accessible to us

2. Third-Party Services

2.1 Website Hosting

Our website is hosted on Netlify and GitHub Pages. These services may collect standard web server logs (like IP address, browser type) for security and operational purposes.

2.2 Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads, which uses cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt-out of personalized ads through Google's Ads Settings.

2.3 Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and usage patterns. This helps us improve our tools and user experience.

3. Cookies

We use cookies in the following ways:

3.1 Essential Cookies

These are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas.

3.2 Analytics Cookies

These help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

3.3 Advertising Cookies

Set by our advertising partners to deliver relevant ads and track ad performance.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

4. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your personal data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete. Any data created while using our tools (like filled templates) remains on your device and can be cleared by you.

If you believe we have unintentionally collected your personal information, please contact us immediately at support@productivitytool.in and we will promptly address your concerns.

5. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date below.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@productivitytool.in
  • Through our website: Contact Form
